# Customer Guide

## What is CLINK?

CLINK is a **Nostr-native Bitcoin Lightning payment protocol** that uses static payment codes (`noffer1...`). Instead of generating a new invoice for each payment, the merchant publishes a persistent offer code that any compatible wallet can pay.

## Paying with CLINK

### Step 1: At Checkout

Select **Bitcoin Lightning (CLINK)** as your payment method and complete the order.

### Step 2: Get the Offer Code

The storefront displays:
- A **CLINK offer string** (`noffer1...`)
- The **amount in sats**
- An **expiry timer**

### Step 3: Pay with Your Wallet

1. Open a **CLINK-compatible wallet** (ShockWallet, ZEUS, or any CLINK-enabled wallet)
2. **Scan** the offer QR code or **paste** the `noffer1...` string
3. The wallet resolves the payment details via Nostr relay
4. Confirm the payment in your wallet

### Step 4: Confirmation

The storefront automatically detects the payment and shows a confirmation message.

## Compatible Wallets

Any wallet that supports the CLINK protocol (`noffer1...` bech32 strings):

- **ShockWallet** — Full CLINK support
- **ZEUS** — Lightning node management with CLINK
- **Other CLINK-enabled wallets** — Check wallet documentation

## Payment Details

- **Protocol**: Nostr Kind 21001 events with NIP-44 encryption
- **Expiry**: Default 30 minutes (merchant-configurable)
- **No account required**: Pay directly from any Lightning wallet
- **Private**: Payment details are encrypted via NIP-44

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Offer not showing | Refresh the page or click "Retry" |
| Offer expired | Click "Generate New Offer" to create a new one |
| Wallet can't scan | Copy the `noffer1...` string and paste it manually |
| Payment not confirming | Wait a few seconds; the storefront polls every 3 seconds |
